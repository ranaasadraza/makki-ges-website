About Us

Makki General Engineering Services is a Pakistan-based construction company headquartered in Shorkot. Committed to delivering world-class engineering and construction solutions, the company was established in 2025 to bring innovation and excellence to the local industry.

We leverage the latest technologies and best practices to turn your vision into reality. Our experienced team of engineers, architects, and project managers ensures that every project—from residential developments to commercial complexes is executed with precision, safety, and sustainability. At Makki General Engineering Services, we prioritize quality craftsmanship, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Through advanced engineering and meticulous project management, we have earned the trust of clients across Pakistan.
